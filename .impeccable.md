## Design Context

### Users
Tabletop RPG game master (and possibly players) tracking a Colonial Marine squad across Aliens RPG campaign sessions. Used during or between game sessions, likely on a laptop in a dim room.

### Brand Personality
Dark, tactical, cinematic. Weyland-Yutani corporate terminal meets field ops dashboard.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark theme mandatory. Amber/green CRT-inspired accent tones on deep gray surfaces. Semi-transparent overlays for status layers. Military UI density — data-forward, no wasted space. Red for death/danger, green for operational, amber for warnings and interactive elements. The interface should feel like something bolted into a dropship console.

### Design Principles
1. **Data density over decoration** — every pixel earns its place, no filler
2. **Color is semantic** — green=operational, amber=caution, red=critical/dead
3. **Tension through restraint** — dark surfaces, muted chrome, sharp accents only where they matter
4. **Military hierarchy** — uppercase tracked labels for headers, monospace for data, clear rank between elements
5. **Feedback is immediate** — every interaction confirms itself visually
